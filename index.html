About Us We love dogs!

Here at RateMyDog, we are a community of dog lovers all around the world. By creating this site we hope to bring people who have the same interest (in dogs) together.

This all started when our founder wanted to accumulate all the dogs and dog owners in the world to provide each other with advice on how to raise dogs. However as there are many sites with this information, rate my dog was created as a fun way to interact with other people and give constructive advice on raising dogs.

As we have given you, the user, the opportunity to rate other people's dogs - We hope you respect that these are real dogs with real owners and be kind on our website.
